## 🔄 Workflow Process

### **Automated Workflow Steps:**

1. **📊 Inventory Monitoring**
   - System continuously monitors inventory levels
   - Calculates days until stockout based on consumption patterns
   - Triggers workflow when threshold (15 days) is reached

2. **📨 Quotation Request**
   - Automatically generates quotation requests
   - Sends emails to preferred vendors
   - Tracks response times and completion rates

3. **📝 Vendor Response Processing**
   - Collects and validates vendor quotations
   - Applies scoring algorithms (price, delivery, quality, reliability)
   - Identifies best vendor options

4. **👨‍💼 Owner Approval**
   - Routes high-value purchases to owner approval
   - Auto-approves low-risk purchases within thresholds
   - Provides detailed justification and budget impact

5. **📄 Purchase Order Generation**
   - Creates professional purchase orders
   - Sends to selected vendors via email
   - Initiates delivery tracking

6. **🚚 Fulfillment Tracking**
   - Monitors delivery status
   - Updates inventory upon receipt
   - Generates reports and analytics
